## Why Is Crossfade Currently Disabled?

If you are wondering why the crossfade feature is currently disabled, the reason is related to stability and performance optimization.

To ensure that crossfade works smoothly and without causing playback issues, I need to thoroughly inspect and review the audio engine, along with several hundred thousand — potentially even millions — of lines of underlying code. This process involves identifying compatibility issues, testing audio transitions, optimizing performance, and ensuring that the feature does not introduce bugs or instability into the application.

Because of the complexity of the system, this investigation and development process takes a significant amount of time.

At the moment, there is unfortunately no estimated timeframe for when the crossfade feature will be re-enabled. The feature will only return once it can operate reliably and provide a stable user experience.
